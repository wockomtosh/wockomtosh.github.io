Games

Back Off Me Booty

Game Link: https://omgitscookietime.itch.io/back-off-me-booty

Role: Controller/Systems Engineer

Team size: 16

Development Time: 4 months

Back Off Me Booty is an alt controller cooperative on-rails shooter developed using Unreal Engine. Players use a seesaw controller to change the tilt of a pirate ship and fire the cannons to protect their loot from enemies. I was responsible for programming the controller, processing controller inputs, creating the level flow, and engineering the environment.

Deep Sea Defender

Game Link: wockomtosh.itch.io/deep-sea-defender

Role: AI/Gameplay Engineer

Team size: 9

Development Time: 4 weeks

Deep Sea Defender is a prototype developed in Unreal Engine. It is a top-down shooter with built-in Twitch integration. When Twitch mode is enabled the Twitch Chat can control the boss AI. I was one of three programmers on the project. I focused on enemy AI, general game flow, UI, menus, and helping with Twitch functionality.

Show Yourself

Game Link: omgitscookietime.itch.io/show-yourself

Role: Engineer

Team size: 5

Development Time: 4 weeks

This is a VR prototype developed using the Unity engine. I was one of two programmers on the project. This was meant to be a serious game based on Carl Jung's statement, "You are what you do, not what you say you'll do." The player is presented with a quiz, after which they can make choices in different scenarios.
